Cloudr is an unofficial client for CloudApp (http://www.getcloudapp.com).

Features:
- share images from a file manager or the Gallery
- share bookmarks
- navigate and browse your drops collection
- delete drops

Stay tuned, more features are on the way.

Cloudr is comletetely free of charge, as will future versions of Cloudr. 

Note that this application requires a (free) CloudApp account.
